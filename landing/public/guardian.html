Guardian Angel — operator notes

Tone and routine hints only. The ethical kernel (MalAbs → … → will) is unchanged. Not a medical or legal authority.

What ships in the repo

  • KERNEL_GUARDIAN_MODE — protective phrasing in LLMModule.communicate.
  • KERNEL_GUARDIAN_ROUTINES + KERNEL_GUARDIAN_ROUTINES_PATH — optional JSON list of care routines (see tests/fixtures/guardian/routines.json).
  • KERNEL_CHAT_INCLUDE_GUARDIAN_ROUTINES — expose guardian_routines in WebSocket JSON (id + title only).

Environment summary

VariableRole
