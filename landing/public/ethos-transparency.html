Short summary for operators and integrators. Full detail lives in the repository docs linked below.

What this system is

Ethos Kernel is a research / reference ethical decision stack (MalAbs hard stops, perception bounds, scoring, memory, optional governance mocks). It is not certified safety equipment, medical advice, or legal compliance software.

What the thresholds cover (honest)

LayerRole
Lexical MalAbsConservative blocks on listed patterns; does not catch all paraphrases.
Semantic MalAbs (default on; KERNEL_SEMANTIC_CHAT_GATE=0 for lexical-only)Embedding / optional LLM arbiter — still heuristic; thresholds trade FP/FN.
Perception & kernelClamped signals and tunable deliberation — not ground truth under attack.
Judicial / DAO mocksDemos only; no replacement for human legal process.

Appeals

Open-source deployments have no built-in appeals workflow. Operators should publish contact and a review process for false blocks. Production systems should define human or second-line review and log overrides (e.g. audit chain).

Auditability

When KERNEL_AUDIT_CHAIN_PATH is set, MalAbs and kernel block events append to a hash-chained JSONL log (optional HMAC). Responses may include malabs_trace when KERNEL_CHAT_INCLUDE_MALABS_TRACE=1.
